## Phase Goal

**As any** staff member (Maker, Checker, or Admin), **I want to** view and export donation summary reports (total amount, receipt count, monthly/daily breakdown) for a date range, **so that** I can see donation activity transparently without needing access to donor PII.

<objective>
Implement the donation summary report for FR-32 / SC#3: a PII-free aggregate over issued donations (total, count, month/day breakdown) exposed to all staff, exportable via the shared xlsx/csv writer, with no RBAC gate and no decrypt path.

Purpose: SC#3 — staff can view donation summary reports by date range and total amount.
Output: `internal/report` service/handler/model; report routes in main.go with NO role gate; E2E subtests including a Maker-can-access assertion.
</objective>
