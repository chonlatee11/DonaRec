## Phase Goal

**As a** Checker or Admin, **I want to** mark issued receipts as keyed into e-Donation (individually or in bulk) and see an aging view of what is still unkeyed against the monthly deadline, **so that** no record is keyed twice or dropped past the 5th-of-next-month cutoff.

<objective>
Implement the keyed-status flag write path and the aging view for FR-31 / SC#2: bulk and per-row mark/unmark with per-record audit, plus a pure Bangkok-aware deadline computation grouping unkeyed issued records into three aging buckets using the config-driven near_due threshold.

Purpose: Close the workflow loop started by export — the person who exports is the person who marks "keyed", and the aging view surfaces what is still outstanding.
Output: `internal/edonation/aging.go` (pure functions), SetKeyed + Aging service methods, handlers, routes on edonationGroup, E2E subtests.
</objective>
