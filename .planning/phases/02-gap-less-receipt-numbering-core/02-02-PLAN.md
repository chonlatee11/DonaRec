    fiscalYear(issueDate time.Time) int:
    - Sep 30 2025 23:59:59 Asia/Bangkok → 2568
    - Oct 1 2025 00:00:00 Asia/Bangkok → 2569
    - Sep 30 2025 17:00:00 UTC (= Oct 1 00:00 BKK) → 2569 (input timezone normalized to BKK)
    - Jan 1 2026 00:00:00 BKK → 2569
    - Sep 30 2026 23:59:59 BKK → 2569
    - Oct 1 2026 00:00:00 BKK → 2570
    Rule: month >= October → CE year + 544; else CE year + 543. Never calls time.Now().

    formatReceiptNo(fiscalYear int, runningNo int, separator string, padding int, yearFormat string, prefix string) string:
    - (2569, 123, "/", 6, "BE4", "") → "2569/000123"
    - (2569, 1000000, "/", 6, "BE4", "") → "2569/1000000"  (D-29: padding is min width, no truncation/error)
    - (2569, 5, "-", 4, "BE4", "HOSP") → "HOSP2569-0005"
    - yearFormat "CE4" → render (fiscalYear - 543) zero-padded to 4
    Uses fmt.Sprintf("%0*d", padding, runningNo) for natural expansion.
  </behavior>
